Why It Matters

1 in 6 children have a developmental disability

Families raising children with sensory processing challenges, autism, ADHD, and other specific needs often lack access to the tools and environments that help their children self-regulate. Without support, everyday moments become overwhelming — for the child and the whole family.

Our Story

We are the connection

Our name comes from the synapse — the connection point between neurons. We see ourselves as that bridge between families and the sensory support they need.

The Synapsory was founded by Terrie Desloge after her experience raising her daughter Faith, who has a developmental disability. Terrie saw firsthand how isolating it can be for families when communities aren't equipped to include children with specific needs.

What started with a handful of calming kits has grown into a movement spanning sensory rooms, professional training, and a community of partners across the St. Louis area.
